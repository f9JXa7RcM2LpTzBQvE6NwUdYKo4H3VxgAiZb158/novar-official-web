About NOVAR

What is NOVAR app

NOVAR app is a free African educational platform for primary and high school learners, where top creators deliver high-quality content and innovative learning solutions to tackle educational inequality and the psychosocial challenges learners face.

Our Mission

To provide free, accessible, and high-quality education to learners across Africa, especially those in rural and under-resourced communities.

What We Offer

Free High-Quality Learning

NOVAR offers engaging, curriculum-aligned content created by top educators and creators. All learning is completely free and accessible to every African learner.

Community & Support

Learners connect in safe, vibrant communities where they ask questions, get help, and grow together. NOVAR builds a culture of ambition, motivation, and academic support.

Skills for the Future

Through AI tools, digital skills content, and career guidance, NOVAR prepares young people for the future that the world is headed to. Learners gain the knowledge and confidence needed to succeed in the real world.

Key Leaders

Education Leaders who support NOVAR

Discover the powerful features that make NOVAR app an exceptional learning platform

Amelia Beattie

Amelia Beattie is the original believer in NOVAR's founders, Shibambu Marvel and Malungelo Mathonsi supporting them when they were building the app from a small room in Gumbani Village and on the edge of shutting down.

She played a pivotal role in securing a R1 million investment from Liberty through the #DriveHope initiative, publicly championing NOVAR as African-born, future-facing, and preparing youth for tomorrow's economy.

Amelia aligned NOVAR with Liberty's vision that education is the most powerful driver of long-term economic freedom, and she and her team have continued to guide, guard, and journey with NOVAR, helping transform a village-built idea into a continent-shaping movement.

Liberty Group SA

Liberty Group is a steadfast partner in NOVAR's journey, providing far more than financial support. Through its #DriveHope initiative, Liberty pledged R1 million to scale operations and keep education free for learners, while connecting the founders to mentors, SME coaches, and professional resources. They provided office space at Braamfontein and brand-new equipment, giving NOVAR the tools to innovate and grow. Liberty champions the app as a perfect match for its education-driven impact strategy, committing to a long-term partnership that guides, empowers, and strengthens NOVAR as it transforms education across South Africa.
